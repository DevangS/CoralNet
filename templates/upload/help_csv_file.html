Each CSV row should correspond to one image, and each column (or comma-separated field) is one piece of metadata.

The metadata fields should be in this order:

  • Filename
  • Date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
  • Location value 1
  • Location value 2
  • ... Location value n
  • Height (centimeters)
  • Latitude
  • Longitude
  • Depth
  • Camera
  • Photographer
  • Water quality
  • Strobes
  • Framing gear
  • White balance

For example, suppose your source has 4 location keys, and you have an image where you want to specify just the date, 4 location values, height in centimeters, and the photographer:

DSC_4289.JPG,2010-01-14,sharkPoint,10m,3,qu4,50,,,,,John Smith,,,,

When you click the "Start upload" button, a preview of the CSV data will appear. You can then confirm whether or not your metadata was entered correctly.
