Each CSV row should correspond to one image, and each comma-separated field is one piece of metadata.

The metadata fields should be in this order:

  • Filename
  • Date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
  • Location value 1
  • Location value 2 (if applicable)
  • Location value 3 (if applicable)
  • Location value 4 (if applicable)
  • Location value 5 (if applicable)
  • Height coverage in centimeters
  • Latitude
  • Longitude
  • Depth
  • Camera
  • Photographer
  • Water quality
  • Strobes
  • Framing gear
  • White balance

All fields must be present, but any field except the filename field may be left blank. For example, if you want to specify just the date, 4 location values, height in centimeters, and the photographer:

DSC_4289.JPG,2010-01-14,sharkPoint,10m,3,qu4,,50,,,,,John Smith,,,,

When you click the "Start upload" button, a preview of the CSV data will appear. You can then confirm whether or not your metadata was entered correctly.
