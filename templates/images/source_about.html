Sources

A Source is a collection of photos or images. To get started with photo uploading, create or join a Source first, and then you can upload your coral photos to the Source. Generally, a Source will contain all of a research group's photos from a particular world location. You can choose to make your photos private or open to the public.

Once you've uploaded your photos to a Source, they'll be ready for analysis. With an Annotation Effort, the photos' contents can be analyzed for the presence and quantity of coral, algae, or anything else.
